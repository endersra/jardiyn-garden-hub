# personas.md — User Personas

## Persona 1: Sarah, the Novice Homeowner

**Goals:**
- Create a beautiful backyard without hiring expensive landscapers
- Learn what to plant in her climate
- Keep plants alive (currently fails with most)

**Workflows:**
- Takes photos of struggling plants
- Searches "why are my plants dying?"
- Wants clear, actionable next steps

**JarDIYn Touchpoints:**
- Plant ID (photo of wilting tomato)
- DIY Report (5 seasonal priorities)
- Chat assistant (quick Q&A)

## Persona 2: Mike, the Eco Enthusiast

**Goals:**
- Create a native plant garden for pollinators
- Reduce water usage
- Support local biodiversity

**Workflows:**
- Researches native plants online (tedious)
- Reads gardening books (often generic)
- Wants to know: "What can I plant this season?"

**JarDIYn Touchpoints:**
- Design copilot (native plant palette)
- Seasonal calendar (frost dates)
- DIY Report (organic pest management)

## Persona 3: Lisa, the Professional Designer

**Goals:**
- Clients can visualize designs before approval
- Communicate with homeowners about CAD
- Create sustainable landscapes

**Workflows:**
- Meets with clients (communication barrier)
- Creates designs in AutoCAD (overkill for simple yards)
- Needs client feedback on mockups

**JarDIYn Touchpoints:**
- Design copilot (quick client mockups)
- CAD export (handoff to professional tools)
- Agent trace (transparency in recommendations)

## Persona 4: James, the Curious Tinkerer

**Goals:**
- Understand how AI reasons about gardens
- Experiment with spatial intelligence
- Learn agentic AI concepts

**Workflows:**
- Reads about AI/ML
- Tests new tools for spatial understanding
- Wants to see agent reasoning

**JarDIYn Touchpoints:**
- Agent trace panel (transparency)
- Chat assistant (testing edge cases)
- Design copilot (seeing how reasoning works)
