# prd.md — JarDIYn Product Requirements Document

## Pain Points (Extracted from Research)

1. **Precision Gap** — Traditional landscaping creates discrepancies between design intent and final output
2. **Visualization Deficit** — Homeowners can't picture what a design will look like before committing
3. **Knowledge Barrier** — Soil types, plant zones, and maintenance schedules are opaque
4. **Sustainability Blindspot** — Conventional practices prioritize aesthetics over ecological impact
5. **Stakeholder Disengagement** — Professionals and homeowners lack shared tools for collaboration

## Use Cases (Prioritized)

### Tier 1: High Impact, Feasible Now (v25 ✅)
- **UC-01 Design Copilot** — Text → 2D layout + plant palette
- **UC-02 DIY Report** — Profile → personalized seasonal brief
- **UC-03 Plant ID** — Photo → species + diagnosis + organic remedy
- **UC-04 Chat Assistant** — Context-aware Q&A grounded in profile

### Tier 2: High Impact, Moderate Complexity (v1.5)
- **UC-05 Smart Watering** — Weather + soil → adaptive irrigation plan
- **UC-06 Seasonal Calendar** — Zone + plants → 12-month timeline

### Tier 3: Future Roadmap (v2.0)
- **UC-07 AR Preview** — Real-time plant overlay on camera
- **UC-08 Community Sharing** — Social + AI remix for other zones
- **UC-09 Ecological Scorer** — Biodiversity + water + carbon impact

## Product Requirements

| Requirement | Rationale | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Zone-accurate recommendations | Users in Zone 3 need different plants than Zone 9 | ✅ |
| Organic-first pest management | Sustainable vision requires no harmful chemicals | ✅ |
| Native plant prioritization | Biodiversity and ecological resilience | ✅ |
| Agentic transparency | Users trust AI reasoning they can see | ✅ |
| Offline capability | Works in yards without reliable internet | ✅ |

| **Homeowner** | Can't design sustainably | AI copilot grounds recommendations in zone/soil | Save 10+ hours planning |
| **Novice Gardener** | Plants keep dying | Plant ID + diagnosis + organic remedies | Reduce plant loss 50% |
| **Designer** | Clients can't articulate | Consumer-friendly design tool | Faster client approval |

## Scope

**In Scope (v25):**
- Profile setup (zone, soil, sun, goals)
- Plant & pest identification
- Garden design copilot
- Seasonal DIY reports
- Conversational assistant
- Garden history timeline
- Offline PWA
- Report export

**Out of Scope (v25):**
- Real smart home control
- Community social features
- Payment processing
- Mobile app (web PWA only)
- Real-time notifications
